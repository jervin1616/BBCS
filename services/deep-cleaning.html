Beyond Standard Cleaning

What Is a Deep Cleaning Service?

A deep cleaning service in Wilmington, NC goes far beyond your regular maintenance clean. While routine cleaning keeps surfaces tidy, deep cleaning targets the accumulated grime, grease, bacteria, and buildup that regular cleaning simply can't reach.

Think inside the oven, behind the refrigerator, inside bathroom tile grout, the tops of cabinets, under furniture, light fixtures, and every baseboard in every room. That's a deep clean — and it's the reset your home deserves.

We recommend a deep cleaning service at least twice a year, before and after major events, or as a one-time clean before starting a recurring maintenance plan with Busy B's.

Deep Clean Checklist

What's Covered in a Busy B's Deep Clean

Every deep cleaning service includes all the following — the areas that only a thorough professional clean can reach.

Kitchen Deep Clean

  • Inside oven, oven racks, and broiler pan
  • Stovetop burner grates degreased
  • Refrigerator interior — shelves, drawers, walls
  • Microwave inside and out
  • Hood vent filter degreased
  • Inside all cabinets and drawers
  • Backsplash scrubbed
  • Sink and disposal sanitized
  • Floor scrubbed (including under appliances where accessible)

Bathroom Deep Clean

  • Grout scrubbed in shower, tub, and floor
  • Soap scum removed from all glass and tile
  • Toilet deep-cleaned inside bowl, under rim, base
  • Exhaust fan cover washed
  • Inside medicine cabinet
  • Caulk lines cleaned

Whole-Home Deep Clean

  • Ceiling fans and light fixtures dusted and wiped
  • All baseboards wiped throughout
  • Door frames and door tops
  • Window sills and window tracks
  • Blinds dusted and wiped
  • Behind and under furniture (where accessible)
  • Light switches and outlet covers sanitized
  • Vents and air return covers dusted
  • Walls spot-cleaned
