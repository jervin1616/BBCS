The Turnover Checklist

What We Do on Every Airbnb Turnover

Kitchen

  • All dishes washed, dried, and put away
  • Countertops sanitized, appliances wiped
  • Stovetop and microwave cleaned inside and out
  • Refrigerator checked — expired items removed
  • Floor swept and mopped
  • Trash emptied and bag replaced

Bathrooms

  • Toilet sanitized thoroughly inside and out
  • Shower/tub scrubbed, glass polished
  • Sink and vanity wiped and sanitized
  • Mirrors polished streak-free
  • Fresh towels staged (or with linen service: laundered)
  • Toiletries restocked per your inventory

Bedrooms

  • Beds stripped, fresh linens staged (linen service available)
  • Pillows fluffed and arranged to hotel standard
  • All surfaces dusted
  • Floors vacuumed and/or mopped
  • Closets and drawers checked for left items

Living Spaces

  • All surfaces dusted and wiped
  • Floors vacuumed and mopped
  • Couches spot-cleaned, cushions arranged
  • TV remotes, controllers wiped and in place
  • Entryway swept and welcoming
