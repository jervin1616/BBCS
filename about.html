29 Years in Medicine
Brandy's Story

From Medicine to a Mission of Cleanliness

Brandy Sellers is a lifelong Wilmington, North Carolina native who dedicated 29 remarkable years to the medical field — serving patients and families through some of life's hardest moments with compassion and professionalism.

When she suffered the heartbreaking loss of her beloved mother, Gloria, Brandy found herself at a crossroads, unable to return to medicine. In her search for healing, she discovered an unexpected peace and deep sense of purpose in cleaning. She found that transforming a space — making it fresh, bright, and welcoming — brought her genuine joy and gave her a way to care for others in a new way.

From that grief, Busy B's Cleaning Service LLC was born. What started as a personal journey of healing became a thriving business that serves hundreds of families and businesses across the Cape Fear region.

💛 In Memory of Gloria

Every clean we do honors the memory of Brandy's mother. Gloria's spirit lives on in every spotless kitchen, every freshly made bed, every home we help make a little brighter.

Mike's Service

21 Years of Service — Now Serving Wilmington

Mike Sellers proudly served 21 years in the United States Coast Guard, protecting America's coastlines and the people who depend on them. His military career instilled in him an uncompromising commitment to excellence, reliability, and getting the job done right — every single time.

When Mike retired from the Coast Guard, he didn't retire from service. He joined Brandy in building Busy B's Cleaning Service, bringing military-grade attention to detail and a deep respect for clients' homes and businesses to every job.

Together, Brandy and Mike have been married for over 12 years. They are proud parents to two children and grandparents to three wonderful grandsons. Their family also includes two energetic German Shorthaired Pointers.

🎖️ Veteran-Owned, Community-Proud

Busy B's Cleaning Service is a proud veteran-owned small business. We believe in supporting our military community and giving back to the people who make Wilmington, NC such a special place to live.

Giving Back

Community is at the Heart of Everything We Do

We believe business should be a force for good. That's why we actively support organizations that make a difference in our community.

🎖️

Wounded Warriors Project

We actively support wounded veterans and their families through our partnership with the Wounded Warriors Project. Mike's 21 years of service made this cause personal — we give back to those who sacrificed so much.

🏠

Domestic Violence Shelters

We donate cleaning services and supplies to local domestic violence shelters in the Wilmington area, helping ensure that survivors have a clean, safe, dignified space to begin rebuilding their lives.

🤲

Homeless Outreach

We partner with local homeless outreach organizations, contributing cleaning services and products to help maintain hygienic conditions for our most vulnerable neighbors.

💪

Addiction Recovery Centers

We provide complimentary cleaning services to addiction recovery centers in the area, supporting the dignity and healing environment that are so essential to recovery. A clean space is a healing space.
