The Complete Room-by-Room Airbnb Cleaning Checklist

Kitchen Cleaning Checklist

The kitchen is where guests spend a lot of time and where cleanliness is most visible. Pay particular attention to:

  • Countertops — wipe down all surfaces, including behind the coffee maker and toaster
  • Stovetop and burners — remove and clean grates; check for grease buildup
  • Microwave — clean inside and out; check the turntable plate
  • Refrigerator — remove all left-behind guest items; wipe shelves and drawers
  • Dishwasher — run empty if needed; ensure dishes are clean and put away
  • Sink — scrub and polish; check the disposal drain
  • Cabinet fronts — wipe down, especially around handles where fingers touch most
  • Floor — sweep thoroughly, then mop; move the trash can to clean underneath
  • Trash — empty and replace liner; wipe inside of trash can

Pro tip from the Busy B's team: Always check inside the microwave last — it's easy to re-splatter it during cooking if you heat up the kitchen first. Clean it after everything else in the kitchen is done.

Bathroom Cleaning Checklist

Bathrooms are where guests are most critical about cleanliness. A single hair, a water spot on the mirror, or a ring in the toilet bowl can kill a 5-star review. Be thorough:

  • Toilet — clean under the rim, inside the bowl, around the base, and behind the tank. Use a fresh toilet brush each turnover if possible
  • Shower and tub — scrub all tile and grout; remove soap scum from glass; check the drain for hair
  • Vanity and sink — wipe, polish, and sanitize; check the drain
  • Mirror — polish streak-free with glass cleaner
  • Floor — sweep and mop; get the corners
  • Fresh towels — replace with clean, folded, hotel-style towels (see linen tips below)
  • Toiletries — restock shampoo, conditioner, soap, and any welcome amenities you provide
  • Exhaust fan cover — wipe dust off the cover every few turnovers

Bedroom Cleaning Checklist

Bedrooms need to feel fresh, inviting, and genuinely clean — not just made-up. Guests who lay down in freshly laundered linens that smell clean have a completely different experience than those who encounter stale sheets that were merely re-made.

  • Strip the bed completely — remove all linens including mattress protector if soiled
  • Inspect mattress — check for stains and flip or rotate if needed
  • Make bed hotel-style — tight corners, pillows fluffed and arranged
  • Dust all surfaces — nightstands, dresser, headboard, ceiling fan
  • Closets and drawers — check for left-behind guest items
  • Windows and blinds — wipe sills; spot-check blinds for dust
  • Floor — vacuum thoroughly including under the bed

Living Room and Common Areas

  • Dust all surfaces — shelves, TV stand, decorative items
  • Wipe down remotes and electronics — high-touch surfaces that guests notice
  • Spot-clean upholstery — check couch cushions for crumbs and spots
  • Rearrange throw pillows and blankets neatly
  • Vacuum all carpets and rugs
  • Sweep and mop hard floors
  • Check entryway — sweep, mat placed neatly, welcome message if you use one

Linen Tips for Wilmington Airbnb Hosts

Linens are often the most overlooked aspect of Airbnb cleanliness — and one of the most important. At Busy B's, our full linen service is the feature our clients value most. Here's why, and how to manage it yourself:

  • Have two sets per bed, minimum — this allows you to always have clean linens ready while the used ones wash
  • Wash in hot water — for sanitization, especially for beach rentals where sand and sunscreen are common
  • Use white or light linens — easier to launder to a high standard; stains are immediately visible and treatable
  • Iron or steam pillowcases — a small step that dramatically elevates the perceived quality of your property
  • Consider a linen service — for hosts managing multiple properties or tight turnover windows, a professional linen service (like the one we offer) saves hours and ensures consistent quality

Restocking Essentials Between Guests

Part of a great Airbnb experience is arriving to a property that's stocked and ready. Use this restocking checklist:

  • Toilet paper (at least 2-4 rolls per bathroom)
  • Paper towels (one full roll in the kitchen)
  • Dish soap and sponge
  • Shampoo, conditioner, and bar or liquid soap
  • Trash bags (one in each can, extras in a labeled spot)
  • Coffee and coffee filters if you provide a coffee maker
  • Salt, pepper, and basic spices if your listing promises them
  • Welcome note or card (a personal touch that guests genuinely appreciate)

How to Get Consistent 5-Star Cleanliness Reviews in Wilmington

After hundreds of Airbnb cleans in the Wilmington area, here's what we've learned separates consistently 5-star-rated properties from those that hover around 4:

  1. Use the same checklist every single time — consistency prevents the missed toilet base or forgotten microwave interior that tanks a review
  2. Smell matters as much as sight — a visually clean space that smells musty is a 3-star review waiting to happen. Use eco-friendly, lightly scented cleaning products and ensure good ventilation
  3. Make beds hotel-style — guests judge bedroom quality immediately. Tight corners and fluffed pillows signal professionalism
  4. Check under and behind things — the dust bunny under the bed, the crumbs behind the microwave, the stray hair on the bathroom floor. Guests explore thoroughly, especially on first stays
  5. Hire a professional for turnovers — especially during peak beach season when same-day turnovers stack up. A professional team with a proven system is more consistent than self-cleaning under time pressure
