Our Story

Born from Loss, Built on Purpose

Brandy Sellers is a lifelong Wilmington native who dedicated 29 years of her life to the medical field. When she suffered the heartbreaking loss of her mother, Gloria, she found herself unable to continue in medicine. In her search for healing, she discovered peace and purpose in the art of cleaning.

From that grief, Busy B's Cleaning Service was born — a company rooted in care, compassion, and an unwavering commitment to making life a little brighter for every client she serves.

Brandy is joined by her husband Mike, who proudly served 21 years in the U.S. Coast Guard. Together, they bring military precision and heartfelt dedication to every home and business they clean. Married for over 12 years, they are proud parents to two children and grandparents to three grandsons.
